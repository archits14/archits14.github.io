ABOUT

Through a tried and tested combination of yoga, powerful breathing exercises, meditation and practical wisdom, the Art of Living Happiness Program will help you live life the way you’ve always wanted to: be calm, confident & armed with the tools you need to handle different thoughts & emotions.

Inspired by the transformation in his life through the Art of Living Youth Empowerment programs, Archit has taken up mentoring & training the country’s youth to make them empowered future leaders. He is an avid reader, teachers of various interesting subjects & loves to meet new people.
